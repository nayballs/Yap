# Demo take — teleprompter script

Open this on your second screen. Notepad front and center on the main screen.
Run `scripts\record-demo.ps1`, press **Enter** to start, then work through the
three beats. Total ~40 seconds. Don't rush — pauses read as confidence.

> Your dictation hotkey is the **`8`** key.

---

## Beat 1 — what Yap is  *(press `8`, read, press `8`)*

> "Hello — this is Yap. It's free, open-source dictation for Windows that runs
> completely offline. Everything happens locally on your PC — your voice never
> touches the cloud. Press one key, talk, and it types into any app."

*Wait for the text to land in Notepad. Give it a beat so viewers see it.*

## Beat 2 — the test  *(press `8`, read WITH the filler words, press `8`)*

Read this one clumsily on purpose — the "um"s and the self-correction are the
whole point, the AI cleanup will strip them on camera:

> "So um, this is me testing the AI cleanup. I went to the shop and uh, I
> couldn't find what I wanted, you know... no wait — I mean, I ended up just
> getting milk."

*Wait for the cleaned text to land.*

## Beat 3 — the payoff  *(press `8`, read, press `8`)*

> "And that's the trick — the ums and the false starts are gone, cleaned up by
> a local AI model. Yap is free, it's on GitHub, and I hope you like it."

*Wait for the text, let it sit for two seconds, then press **Enter** in the
recorder window to stop.*

---

**Retakes are free.** If a beat stumbles, just stop and re-run — the recorder
overwrites `docs/demo.mp4` + `docs/demo.gif` each time.
